Mass-spring system

Choose the number of mass and spring, enter variable and look how the system moves

How does it work?

It's always difficult to do a physical exercise without drawing. Especially when there are multiple variables. I remember myself struggling with my pencil trying to make a worthy drawing. That's why I decided to help those who are in the same situation. This website will simulate a mass spring system or a pendulum system.
You will have to enter different variables, as the weight, the gravity, the constant of rigidity and others.
